Street Tacos

Description

Tacos can be made very different ways, but if you're looking for the aboslute best style of taco, look no further than this street taco recipe. These street tacos takes the basics of tortilla, meat, and cheese, and brings the flavor town with different spices. You can also dress up these tacos nicely as you like with plenty of different garnishments, or leave them as they are with classic meat and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 flat iron steak (or alternative cut of steak or chicken)
  • 1 pkg 6" corn tortillas
  • soy sauce
  • chili powder
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cayenne pepper (optional)
  • shredded cheese (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cut up steak, or desired meat, into 1/4 - 1/2 inch cubes.
  2. In a medium skillet, pour soy sauce until it just covers bottom. Turn on medium heat.
  3. Add cut up meat to skillet and distrbute evenly over bottom of skillet.
  4. sprinkle chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper over meat so it lightly covers all pieces. Stir the meat in the skillet and repeat spices so that each side is lightly coated.
  5. Let cook on medium heat for 10-15 min, or until desired doneness, stirring frequently.
  6. Remove from heat. Place spoonful of meat into tortilla and add cheese, or any other garnish you desire.
  7. Enjoy taco.
