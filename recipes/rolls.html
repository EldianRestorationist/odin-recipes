Roast Beef Rolls

Description

Thinlu sliced roast beef sandwich in rolls, baked with cheese until golden brown.

Ingredients

  • 1lb thinly sliced deli roast beef
  • 1 pkg Hawaiian rolls (12 ct)
  • 1/4cup cream style horseradish
  • 6 slices provolone cheese
  • 1/3cup butter, melted
  • 2tbls Worcestershire sauce
  • 2tsp packed light brown sugar
  • 1/4tsp onion powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees ferenheit
  2. Coat 9x13 inch pan with cooking spray.
  3. Cut rolls in half horizontally. Place bottom half in baking dish.
  4. Spread horseradish on cut side of placed rolls. Add roast beef and cheese.
  5. Close sandwiches and cut into 12 sandwiches.
  6. Combine butter, Worcestershire sauce, sugar and onion powder in small bowl and mix well.
  7. Pour butter mixture evenly over sandwiches. Cover and refridgerate 1 hour to overnight.
  8. Bake, uncovered, for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is melted and rolls are golden brown.
