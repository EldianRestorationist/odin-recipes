Homemade Beanless Chili

Description

This homemade beanless chili uses classic ingredients with a touch of spice. It is good for cold days or a quick dinner. The flavor combination goes great if you want to add cheese, sour cream, jalapenos, or just eat it as is.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 28oz can tomato puree
  • 14oz can beef stock
  • 6oz can tomato paste
  • 2tbls chili powder
  • 2tbls garlic powder
  • 2 tbls onion powder
  • 1 tbls cumin
  • 1 tbls paprika
  • 2tsp salt
  • 2tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in large pan on medium heat until brown.
  2. Drain excess grease. Return ground beef to pan.
  3. Add tomato puree, tomate paste, and beef stock.
  4. Stir in all spices.
  5. On medium heat, bring chili to boil, stirring frequently.
  6. Remove from heat. Enjoy chili.
